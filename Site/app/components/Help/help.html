Frequently Asked Questions

Which name and password should be used for login?

Your surname and password that you use for Windows login.

I entered my name and password, but a popup message appears. What should I do?

If popup has the title "Failed Login" - Please, check the correctness of your login and password.
If popup has the title "Authentication error" - Please, ask your team leader or other user with admin rights to make you active. After your activation press Ctrl+F5 for updating page.
If you are activated but still can't login - Please contact support.
